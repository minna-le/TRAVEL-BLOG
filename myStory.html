My story

I am Minna, an aspiring software developer from Finland and a proud mom of a spirited 3-year-old. I feel fortunate to have my parents, who are still healthy, from Vietnam, along with my beloved sister from the US, who is not only very close to me but also shares my passion for traveling together. Our family frequently travels across continents to reconnect and create cherished memories together.

This blog fulfills my deep desire to preserve our family moments, photos, and experiences. It also serves as a testament to my journey as a software developer, beginning with my passion for crafting user-friendly websites that bring our stories to life.

Through this platform, I hope to inspire families to include their grandparents in their travels, fostering memories that span generations. I believe in the importance of valuing our family connections, sharing unique experiences, and strengthening our bonds while exploring the world together.
